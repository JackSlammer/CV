My Dream of Building of Skoolie and Living on the Road!

Skoolie...Really?!!!

Where have I been while this Skoolie revolution was under way! Didn't even know they existed until I stummbled across a few while looking for affordable RV's. Who knew you could take an old school bus and reinvent it into something totally cool and practical, then give it a wicked name like "Skoolie"!

There are no Skoolie dealerships that I know of, so you have to be ready to:

  1. Build your own from and old school bus you find for sale.
  2. Buy one that someone has already converted that suits yours needs

This subject envolves a lot of research for anyone concidering a Skoolie build. Most people I've come across through watching their videos on Youtube, on average, are taking around a year to be up and running. Now, I didn't say finished. Not sure I found anyone that said the word "Finished". Seems those Skoolies are are always a work in progress and as you go you find one thing after another that needs fixing or improving.
